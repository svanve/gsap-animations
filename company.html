At home everywhere in Europe

DPD Germany is part of the international DPDgroup. The No. 1 on the European parcels market combines innovative technologies with a high level of local expertise, which enables a flexible and convenient service for both shippers and consignees. With a workforce of 77,000 delivery experts and more than 47,000 local Pickup parcelshops, DPDgroup ships 5.3 million parcels a day in over 230 countries. DPDgroup is part of France's Groupe La Poste, Europe's second-largest postal service.

Sustainability at DPD

We are responsible partners to our customers, consignees, employees and the community. With our group-wide sustainability strategy we focus on those areas where we can make a direct difference and which are closely linked to our core business.
